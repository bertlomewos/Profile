    From a young age, I've always been captivated by the world of fantasy - the magical realms, the awe-inspiring dragons, and the endless possibilities that only exist in our wildest dreams. Growing up in Hawassa and Addis Ababa, I was drawn to movies, comics, and media that allowed me to immerse myself in these fantastical concepts.

    My love for creative expression naturally led me to the art of drawing, as a way to bring the visions in my mind to life on paper. However, the challenges of mastering the craft almost led me to give up at times. But just when I was about to abandon the idea, I discovered the world of game development.

    Unlike drawing, which I had to toil over, game development felt like a natural fit. The process of creating interactive digital experiences, where I could manifest any idea or concept, was both exhilarating and rewarding. The sheer possibility of what I could achieve was limitless, and that's when I knew I had found my true passion - to become a game developer.

    The journey has not been without its difficulties; game development is a demanding and often exhausting endeavor. But the joy I find in the process, the endless possibilities, and the ability to share my imaginative creations with others, makes it all worthwhile. This is the path I have chosen, and I am determined to keep exploring the realms of fantasy, one game at a time.
